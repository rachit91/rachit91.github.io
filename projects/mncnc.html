Client

Minnesota Children & Nature Connection
Problem

Due to hosting and platform changes, MN-CNC group needed to create a new website experience (previous data and website repo was lost). The goal of their website is to offer usable resources, promote nature play for children and provide visitors with newsworthy information.

Providing us with a timeline of about 1 month, we had a few constraints as well: Wordpress platform with limited plugins, visual branding and images provided by the client (images weren't of the best quality) and emphasis to be given on news and about the organization.

Role

I was responsible for interaction design and information architecture of the website. I collaborated with a visual designer to produce visuals for the client.

I used Sketch, Keynote, Photoshop (to edit a few images), Balsamiq and InVision for this project.
